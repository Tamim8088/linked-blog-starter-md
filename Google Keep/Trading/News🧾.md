If the News is worse than expected, you buy the market. 
And if the News is better than expected, you sell the market.

1) In any economic calendar when the Actual number is better than Forecast number, it will good for the economy and currency. (Bullish)

2) When the Actual number has no significant change to Forecast number, it causes mixed reaction for the economy and currency.

3) When the Actual number is worse than Forecast number, it will be bad for the economy and currency. (Bearish)
