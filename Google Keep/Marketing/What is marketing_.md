Advertising:
If a circus is coming to town and you put up a sign that's advertising.

Promotion:
If you put the sign on the back of an elephant and walk it into into town that's promotion.

Publicity:
And if the elephant through the mayor's flower bed (publicity stunt) and local news writes a story about it that's publicity. 


Public Relations:
And of course if you get the mayor to laugh about it that's public relations.

Sales:
But if you tell them about the various entertainment booths you will have, the fun they are going to have, answer their questions that's sales. 

And if you planned the entire thing that's marketing.
