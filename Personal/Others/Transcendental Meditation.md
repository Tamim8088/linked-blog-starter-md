Position: 
Time: 5 mins
Music: Eternity 

Being is a bliss in it's nature. It is infinite happiness. 

Mind is always in the direction of greater happiness. 

It is the experience of everyone wherever the mind goes, it goes in the direction of greater happiness and because the nature of inner being is bliss, infinite happiness, therefore the mind during transcendental meditation takes that inward course in a more spontaneous manner.
